## Verse 1 - The Introduction (May 6)

A quiet message crossed my way,<br>
A name I'd never thought I'd say.<br>
No fireworks lit the evening sky,<br>
Just one small chance that wandered by.

I didn't know what time would write,<br>
Or how you'd slowly change my life.<br>
One simple "meet her" changed the page,<br>
Beginning our unwritten stage.

## Pre-Chorus

Who would've known<br>
A single hello<br>
Would become the place<br>
I'd always call home?

## Chorus

From strangers into something true,<br>
One little step led me to you.<br>
Through every silence, every mile,<br>
You gave my weary heart a smile.

Now every dream I hold feels new,<br>
Because I get to dream with you.<br>
From that first day until today,<br>
I'll choose your hand every single day.

## Verse 2 - Finally Talking (May 15)

The words grew longer every night,<br>
The hours somehow felt too light.<br>
We laughed through stories, fears, and dreams,<br>
Like finding calm in rushing streams.

The distance never felt so wide,<br>
Whenever you were by my side.<br>
Screen to screen, yet heart to heart,<br>
You slowly became my favorite part.

## Verse 3 - Courtship (May 20-27)

I asked with hope, but trembling still,<br>
Not knowing what your heart would feel.<br>
Each passing day became a prayer,<br>
Wondering if love was waiting there.

Then finally your answer came,<br>
Forever changing everything.<br>
No grand parade, no crowded room—<br>
Just joy that filled my quiet room.

## Bridge - The Storms

Accounts disappeared,<br>
The distance remained.<br>
Some days were confusing,<br>
Some nights filled with rain.

But none of the battles<br>
Could make me let go.<br>
Because loving you<br>
Is the easiest thing I know.

## Final Chorus

From May until this very day,<br>
You've painted colors through the gray.<br>
The story's only just begun,<br>
Our brightest chapter's yet to come.

No matter where tomorrow leads,<br>
Or how long this long distance will be,<br>
When every mile is finally gone,<br>
I'll still be singing our first song.
